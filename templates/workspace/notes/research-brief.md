## Original Question

Record the user's raw question exactly as asked.

## Refined Question

Write the concise research question that will drive source gathering. Keep this aligned with `config.json.question`.

## Triage Summary

Summarize the question's clarity, scope, domain, stakes, time sensitivity, subjectivity, and the main reason this brief exists.

## User Intent

State what decision, understanding, or deliverable the user actually wants.

## Intended Output

Describe the expected report shape, comparison, recommendation, literature review, or other final artifact.

## Scope

List the included topics, excluded topics, time horizon, geography, and any constraints on evidence or effort.

## Assumptions

Record any assumptions you are using so downstream research does not treat them as user-confirmed facts.

## Clarifications

List targeted clarifying questions and answers. If none were needed, say so explicitly.

## Evidence Plan

State the planned source mix, likely authoritative or scholarly providers, corroboration needs, and any red-flag areas.

## Report Plan

Outline the sections, comparison axes, or evaluation criteria the final report should cover.

## Expected Uncertainty

Note what is likely to remain uncertain, subjective, contested, or time-sensitive even after research.

## Handoff Notes

State whether to continue with `deep-research` or `literature-review`, plus any execution notes for the next skill.
